*David Copperfield*, written by Charles Dickens, is a coming-of-age novel that follows the life journey of David from a troubled childhood to maturity. Through hardships, friendship, love, and perseverance, David learns about kindness, ambition, and integrity. The story is filled with memorable characters — from the loyal Agnes and wise Mr. Micawber to the deceitful Uriah Heep — each shaping his growth. Combining humor, emotion, and social insight, *David Copperfield* reflects Dickens’s own experiences and remains one of his most beloved and autobiographical works.
