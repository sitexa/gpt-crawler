*1984*, written by George Orwell, is a powerful dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society ruled by Big Brother. It follows Winston Smith, a man who secretly rebels against the oppressive regime that controls every aspect of life — even thoughts. Through constant surveillance, propaganda, and fear, the Party destroys truth and individuality. Winston’s struggle for freedom and love becomes a tragic quest for humanity in a world of lies. *1984* is a timeless warning about authoritarianism, manipulation, and the loss of personal freedom.
